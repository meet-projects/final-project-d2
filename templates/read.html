Success Stories

Shlomo is 46 years old, has a diploma in chemistry and in bookkeeping. He is a divorcee and he lives with his mother. Over the years he worked in different jobs and after a severe relapse in his psychiatric symptoms in 2007 he started his vocation rehabilitation journey in Shekulo Tov Group. At the beginning of the training he received vocational rehabilitation training in the manufacture field and few months later he was in charge of the warehouse of the plant. During this time he received training and support from his employment specialist, attended supported employment classes, went to work in different free job markets in order to improve his working skills applied to jobs in the free market. Two years ago he started working in a part time job in the Computer Science Faculty at Technion-the Israel Institute of Technology. According to Shlomo, by working there he closed a circle, since he studied there 20 years ago. Two months ago, in a formal ceremony that included all faculty members and the Dean, Shlomo received a Certificate of Excellence for his work at the Faculty. Naturally he was very excited to receive this acknowledgment and the best part is that he enjoys what he does. Recently Shlomo moved to a full time job.
