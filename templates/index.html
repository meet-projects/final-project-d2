About Us

The integrative plants simulate a production work environment that enables the service users to acquire vocational skills, and serves as an accelerator for their rehabilitation and recovery processes. The plants manufacture chocolate, candles, soaps, paper products and sweets and include a wide variety of training programs both in and out of the plant.
